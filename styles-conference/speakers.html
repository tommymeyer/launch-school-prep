Twenty phenomenal speakers from around the world join us to share their stories and experiences.

Jeff Wells

organIzation: STAYING ORGANIZED STARTS WITH YOU

When life starts piling up it's important to set YOURSELF up for success. Working with non-profits and start-ups, Jeff has experience working in multiple departments simultaneously. Constantly seeing his email inbox in the hundreds he needed to take the time to organize how he worked through his day.

About Jeff

Jeff works at the mattress start-up Tuft & Needle. Working with the supply chain, operations, and engineering teams he is truly a jack of all trades.
